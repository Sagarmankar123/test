# Privacy Policy for Rakshak

**Last Updated**: December 2024

## Introduction

Rakshak ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and web application (collectively, the "App").

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:
- Register for an account
- Use emergency features
- Add trusted contacts

**Personal information includes:**
- Name
- Phone number
- Emergency contact details
- Profile information

### 2. Location Data

With your explicit permission, we collect and use:
- Real-time GPS coordinates
- Location accuracy data
- Address information (reverse geocoded)

**Purpose**: To share your location with trusted contacts during emergencies.

**You can disable location access at any time** through your device settings.

### 3. Device Information

We automatically collect:
- Device type and model
- Operating system
- Browser type
- IP address
- Device identifiers

**Purpose**: To ensure app compatibility and improve performance.

### 4. Usage Data

We collect information about how you use the App:
- Features used
- Emergency alerts triggered
- App interactions
- Error logs and crash reports

**Purpose**: To improve app functionality and user experience.

### 5. Audio Recordings

When you activate an emergency alert, the app may record audio (with your permission):
- **Storage**: Locally on your device
- **Purpose**: Safety evidence during emergencies
- **Retention**: You control deletion
- **Sharing**: Only if you choose to share

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

1. **Provide Emergency Services**
   - Send SOS alerts to your trusted contacts

2. **Improve App Functionality**
   - Fix bugs and errors
   - Enhance user experience
   - Develop new features

3. **Communication**
   - Send important updates
   - Respond to support requests
   - Notify about emergency-related information

4. **Security**
   - Protect against fraud
   - Ensure app security
   - Verify user identity

5. **Legal Compliance**
   - Comply with legal obligations
   - Respond to lawful requests
   - Enforce our terms

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage

**Most data is stored locally on your device:**
- Trusted contacts
- Emergency logs
- User preferences
- Audio recordings
- Settings

**Benefits:**
- You maintain control
- Works offline
- Enhanced privacy
- Fast access

### Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- Data encryption (in transit and at rest)
- Secure authentication
- Regular security audits
- Access controls

**However, no method is 100% secure.** We cannot guarantee absolute security.

### Data Retention

- **Emergency logs**: Stored locally; you can delete anytime
- **Audio recordings**: Stored locally; you control deletion
- **Account data**: Retained until account deletion
- **Usage data**: Anonymized after 90 days
